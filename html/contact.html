Frequently Asked Questions

What types of projects do you work on?

We specialize in documentaries, educational content, commercial videos, event coverage, and training materials. We're always open to discussing unique projects that align with our storytelling mission.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. Simple projects can be completed in 2-4 weeks, while comprehensive documentaries may take 2-6 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you provide equipment and crew?

Yes, we have professional-grade equipment and a skilled crew. We handle all aspects of production from pre-production planning to post-production editing and delivery.

Can you work with our existing content?

We can work with your existing footage, photos, and materials to create compelling narratives. We also offer post-production services for content you've already shot.
